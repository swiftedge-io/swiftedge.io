Web Designer

Deliver the designs for the information organization, aesthetic look and feel, interaction design, and experience flows for specific parts of user experiences through the creation of sketches, storyboards, IA, wireframes, prototypes or high-fidelity visual compositions.

  • Work within an agile team of UX designers and UX researchers to produce effective and delightful interactive experiences.
  • Follow creative direction and existing style guides and frameworks to create sketches, wireframes, user flows, visuals, prototypes and other interface specifications.
  • Present and explain design choices and options based on competitive reviews and gestalt design Principles.
  • Meet quality, usability and accessibility standards and follow team processes for creating, maintaining and optimizing our interaction and visual design patterns and frameworks for web and/or native app experiences.
